Causal hypothesis

The radial vibration drift on bearing housing VT-2031A is consistent with early-stage rolling element bearing fatigue. The signature lacks the characteristic high-frequency components of cage damage or outer race spalling, suggesting the failure is in its initial degradation phase, with an estimated 11–14 days before the action threshold (10.5 mm/s) is breached. [1]

Bearing temperature (TT-2032) has trended upward 6.4°C over the same 28-day window, consistent with increasing friction. [2] Pump operating point (discharge pressure, flow) is nominal, ruling out hydraulic loading as the primary cause. [3]

Most likely root cause

Lubricant degradation. The last documented oil change was 21 months ago (post-overhaul, August 2023). [4] OEM service interval for this pump class under continuous duty is 18 months. Bearing housing temperature signature suggests viscosity loss consistent with oxidation-stage lubricant aging.
